Description

The circular columns counterbalance the rectangular volumes. The large perimeter windows, like the strong expressive faces of the concrete, are moderated by a lower paneling, composing a vertical that offsets with the intent of creating facades in its detachment. The floor plan and its composition evoke infallible modernist Le Corbusian principles by nature: rooftop gardens, open floor plans, free facades, just to mention the most prominent. For a rugged terrain, the response is clear; the workshop, therefore, seeks integrated solutions that obey the immediate context.
